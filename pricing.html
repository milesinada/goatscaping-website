What Affects Your Price?

Terrain

Steep slopes or rocky terrain may require extra time and care. Most hillside jobs are within the standard rate.

Vegetation Density

Heavy overgrowth with thick vines or mature brush takes longer. We'll assess before quoting.

Time & Visits

Dense areas may need multiple visits. We return until the job is done, no surprise charges mid-job.
